Recording Market Prices with the Value Manager Mobile App

Keeping your stock market prices updated is essential for maintaining an accurate view of your portfolio's value and performance.

The Value Manager mobile application allows investors to quickly record market prices directly from their smartphone, making it easy to update portfolio valuations even when away from the desktop application.

Why Recording Market Prices Matters

Stock prices change constantly. Recording updated market prices allows the software to calculate:

  • Current portfolio value
  • Unrealized capital gains or losses
  • Investment performance metrics
  • Portfolio allocation and risk exposure

Without updated prices, portfolio performance calculations may not reflect current market conditions.

Updating Prices from Your Smartphone

The mobile application provides a simple interface where investors can enter the latest market price for any stock in their portfolio.

This makes it possible to update prices immediately after checking market quotes from financial websites, trading platforms, or market news applications.

Quick Price Entry for Multiple Stocks

The app allows investors to navigate through their list of portfolio companies and quickly record new prices.

This streamlined workflow helps maintain updated data with minimal effort, even for portfolios containing many stocks.

Synchronization with the Desktop Software

Once market prices are recorded through the mobile application, they are synchronized with the main portfolio database.

This ensures that both the mobile and desktop applications display the same updated information and portfolio valuation.

Better Investment Decisions

By keeping market prices updated regularly, investors can analyze portfolio performance more accurately and make better-informed investment decisions.

Accurate price data is essential for evaluating investment returns, calculating CAGR, and monitoring dividend-adjusted performance.
