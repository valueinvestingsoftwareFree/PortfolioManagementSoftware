Community Requested Features Under Evaluation

The software already includes advanced stock analysis, intrinsic value estimation, dividend tracking, portfolio management, technical indicators, mobile integration, investor psychology training, and many other professional tools.

The following capabilities are currently being evaluated based on requests, feedback, and ideas submitted by investors and software users.

Future Development Roadmap

1. Dividend Growth Forecast Simulator

Project future dividend income using historical dividend growth rates and multiple assumptions.

2. Retirement Income Planner

Estimate whether future portfolio income can support retirement expenses.

3. Portfolio Stress Testing

Simulate bear markets, recessions, inflation shocks, and dividend cuts.

4. Financial Independence (FIRE) Dashboard

Track progress toward financial independence and estimate years remaining.

5. Net Worth Projection Engine

Forecast future net worth using contributions, reinvestment, and expected returns.

6. Portfolio Rebalancing Planner

Automatically calculate portfolio adjustments needed to maintain target allocations.

7. Goal-Based Investing Dashboard

Track investment progress toward retirement, passive income, education, or wealth goals.

8. Dividend Replacement Risk Analysis

Estimate portfolio income impact if a company reduces or eliminates dividends.

9. Portfolio Dependency Analysis

Identify which investments contribute most to wealth creation and dividend generation.

10. Monte Carlo Portfolio Simulator

Generate thousands of potential future outcomes using randomized market assumptions.

11. Dividend Snowball Simulator

Visualize long-term wealth creation through dividend reinvestment.

12. Portfolio Health Score

Measure diversification, concentration, dividend quality, and overall portfolio health.

13. What-If Scenario Simulator

Evaluate hypothetical decisions before investing real money.
