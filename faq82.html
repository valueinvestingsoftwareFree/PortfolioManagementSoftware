What Counts as a Closed Position?

A closed position is any investment that has been fully sold or exited. This includes selling all shares of a stock, liquidating a portion that reduces the holding to zero, or closing out an entire trade after achieving a target return or stop-loss.

Tracking closed positions is essential because it provides a complete history of capital movement and performance. It shows not only what you currently own, but also how your past decisions have contributed to overall portfolio results.
