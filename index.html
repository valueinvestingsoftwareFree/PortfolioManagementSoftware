Why Not Just Use Excel?

While spreadsheets are flexible, they become difficult to maintain as your portfolio grows. This software was built specifically to solve problems like:

  • Tracking multiple portfolios
  • Managing cost basis accurately
  • Analyzing dividend income
  • Visualizing performance over time
  • Monitoring real total return

It provides structured financial tracking without the complexity of building your own formulas.
